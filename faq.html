Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TVS King Three Wheelers

How many passengers can a TVS King three-wheeler typically carry?
The TVS King Deluxe, a passenger-oriented model, can carry up to four people, including the operator. Standard auto-rickshaws generally have seating for up to three passengers in the back, in addition to the driver.
What fuel options are available for TVS King passenger models?
TVS King passenger models, such as the King Deluxe and King Duramax Plus, are available in multiple powertrain configurations, including LPG, CNG, and petrol. The TVS King EV MAX is our premium electric variant.
What are the engine specifications for passenger variants?
The TVS King Deluxe LPG/CNG variants feature a 4-stroke single-cylinder air-cooled engine (8.98 hp, 13 Nm torque). The petrol variant uses an EFI-based engine (10.46 hp, 15.5 Nm). The TVS King EV MAX uses a high-performance PMSM motor with 11kW peak power and 40Nm peak torque.
What is the range/mileage of the passenger models?
The Petrol model offers approx 28 ± 3 km/l, while CNG provides around 45 ± 5 km/kg. The electric TVS King EV MAX comes with a certified range of 179 km per full charge.
What is the loading capacity of the TVS King Kargo?
The internal combustion TVS King Kargo has a payload capacity of 426 kg. The heavy-duty TVS King Kargo HD EV has a Gross Vehicle Weight (GVW) of 998 kg.
What are the dimensions of the loading deck?
The standard TVS King Kargo features a loading deck of 1500 mm (length) by 1300 mm (width). The HD EV variant boasts a massive 6.6-foot load deck, one of the largest in its segment.
How long does it take to charge the King Kargo HD EV?
The TVS King Kargo HD EV can be charged from 0 to 100% in approximately 3 hours and 10 minutes, making it highly efficient for business operations.
What features are included for driver comfort in Cargo models?
Cargo models come with leaf spring suspension for better load handling, a spacious cabin with fully rolling windows, and TVS SmartXonnect with Bluetooth for navigation and fleet management (on EV models).
